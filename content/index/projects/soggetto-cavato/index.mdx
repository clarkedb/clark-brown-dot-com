Encodes text as an audio motif using <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soggetto_cavato" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" aria-label="External Link">Soggetto Cavato</a> encoding. This maps the vowels of text to audio pitches based on solfege and dates back to the earth 16th century.
